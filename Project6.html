DY-MARK PRINT MATERIALS FOR
AUSTRALIAN MINING REVIEW & THE TRADIE MAGAZINE

Project Summary

I had the opportunity to design highly impactful print materials for Dy-Mark, aimed at strategically elevating their brand presence in both the mining and trade industries. Through carefully crafted designs for the Australian Mining Review and The Tradie Magazine, I ensured that Dy-Mark’s reputation as an industry leader was clearly communicated. This project focused on maximizing brand visibility, aligning with key marketing objectives, and producing professional, visually striking collateral that resonated with the target audience and reinforced Dy-Mark’s position in both industries.
