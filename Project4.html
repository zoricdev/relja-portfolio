AUTOTECH EPOXY FLOOR COATING

Project Summary

As a graphic designer, I developed the packaging for Autotech Epoxy Floor Coating, creating a sleek, modern design that positioned the product as a leader in its category. By focusing on the product's key benefits — such as resistance to hot tire pick-up, oil stain protection, and easy one-coat application for up to 40m² — the design was tailored to resonate with the target audience of professionals and DIY enthusiasts. The packaging's bold and professional aesthetic not only conveyed the product’s durability and high performance but also ensured it stood out on retail shelves, making it the go-to choice for quality-conscious customers.
