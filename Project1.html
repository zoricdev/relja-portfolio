SONIC FINANCIAL SERVICES

Project Summary

In the development of the Sonic Financial Services project, my objective was to design a website that summarizes a sense of refined professionalism and corporate elegance. The goal for this project was to ensure a seamless integration between the website’s visual storytelling and the company’s branding, to nurture a unified brand identity. Cleverly placing CTA’s across the website to generate as many leads as possible for the business. This approach was fundamental in establishing a strong market presence. As a UX/UI designer, my role extended beyond aesthetic considerations; it encompassed the strategic implementation of functionality to enhance user engagement and satisfaction.
